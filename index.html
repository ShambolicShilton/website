About Me

My name is Thomas Shilton and I'm a final year student studying Computer Games Programming (CGP) at the University of Derby. Over the next year, I am focusing on developing a full 3D platformer level, diving deep into gameplay mechanics and level design to make a fun and engaging game (and some extras that I can't reveal yet). I have worked primarily with C++ and Unreal Engine, though I have also explored Unity and built a small 2D platformer. I enjoy tackling the challenges of games programming, whether that's building complex movement logic or spending hours finding one line of code that makes a plane fly backwards. My goal is to broaden my skills beyond Unreal Engine and become a well-rounded games developer. Naturally, when I'm not programming, you'll likely find me playing The Legend of Zelda, Minecraft, or testing all the ways that Mario Kart can infuriate me - and then inevitably coming back for more!
